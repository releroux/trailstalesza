Trails & Tales

This is a travel blog about exploring South Africa — its places, its stories, and its people. It’s about sharing real experiences, adventures, and a bit of local history along the way.

The goal is simple: to show that travel doesn’t need to be perfect, expensive, or out of reach. It’s about the kind of adventures anyone can have — road trips in an old bakkie, weekends away on a budget, and discovering the beauty of South Africa without all the fancy gear.
